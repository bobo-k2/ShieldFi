## Twitter Strategy Overhaul

### What's NOT Working
- Generic educational tweets with no hook → 3 impressions
- "Drop your wallet" engagement bait to 8 followers → nobody sees it
- Corporate-sounding brand voice

### What TO Do Instead
1. **Newsjack aggressively** — every Solana exploit, hack, rugpull = tweet within hours with our analysis
2. **Quote-tweet bigger accounts** — add genuine security insight to popular crypto tweets
3. **Build in public** — "Day 7 of building a Solana wallet scanner. Today I found X..."
4. **Scan celebrity/whale wallets** — publish findings (public data), tag them
5. **Create controversy** — hot takes on wallet security ("Most wallet security tools are theater. Here's why.")

### Tweet Timing
- Post at **3 PM CET** (current cron) for US morning audience
- Add **second post at 10 PM CET** for US afternoon/evening
- Weekends: only newsjack, no scheduled content

### Engagement Cron (NEW)
Daily search for:
- "solana" tweets mentioning wallet/security topics → reply with value
- Quote-tweet trending Solana news with security angle
- Follow accounts in the Solana security space
