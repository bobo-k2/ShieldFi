## Day 4: Educational Thread 🧵

Thread: 5 signs a token in your Solana wallet is a scam 🧵

1/ It appeared without you buying it
→ Airdrop spam. Scammers send millions of worthless tokens to random wallets hoping you'll interact with their site.

2/ The name mimics a real token
→ "USDC" with a different mint address = fake. ShieldFi catches these impersonation attempts automatically.

3/ Mutable metadata
→ The creators can change the token's name, symbol, or image at any time. Today it's "SafeMoon", tomorrow it's "USDT" — same scam token.

4/ No market price or liquidity
→ If you can't sell it, it's either dead or a trap. Some tokens are designed to be bought but never sold (honeypots).

5/ Freeze authority is active
→ The creator can freeze your tokens at will. Legitimate projects renounce this authority.

Scan any wallet for free → shieldfi.app
