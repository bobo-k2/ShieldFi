## Description
ShieldFi is a free, AI-powered security scanner built specifically for Solana wallets.

**The problem:** 98%+ of tokens on Solana are scams, and most wallets are full of fake airdrops, impersonation tokens, and risky approvals that users don't even know about. Wallet Guard shut down. Blowfish was acquired by Phantom. De.Fi doesn't support Solana. There's no standalone security tool for the #2 blockchain.

**How it works:** Paste any Solana wallet address → get an instant risk report. No wallet connect, no signup, no browser extension needed.

**What it checks:**
- 🔍 Token risk analysis (verified / suspicious / fake)
- ⚠️ Fake token detection (catches USDC/SOL impersonators)
- 🔓 Authority analysis (mint, freeze, metadata mutability)
- 📊 AI-powered contract risk scoring
- 🔔 Real-time Telegram alerts for wallet monitoring

**Who it's for:** Solana traders, DeFi users, memecoin degens, anyone who wants to know if their wallet is safe.

Solana-only by design. Deep coverage > shallow multi-chain.
